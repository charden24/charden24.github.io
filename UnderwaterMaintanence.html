We offer a hull cleanings from Destin to Port St Joe Florida, we are base out of Panama City Beach.

Hull Cleaning

We offer monthly hull cleanings. Included in a monthly hull cleaning you get your hull cleaned, all running gear cleaned, zincs inspected, a written report, and photos depending on water clarity.

Pricing For Hull Cleaning

  • Sailboats ($100.00 Minimum) - $2.50/FT Standard, $7.50/FT Heavy, 12.00/FT Extreme
  • PowerBoats ($110.00 Minimum) - $2.75/FT Standard, $8.50/FT Heavy, 14.00/FT Extreme
  • Catamarans ($140.00 Minimum) - $3.25/FT Standard, $9.50/FT Heavy, 16.00/FT Extreme
  • Prop cleaning - $50
  • Dock pilings - call for pricing
  • Monthly hull cleanings are always charged the standard rate
  • Heavy or extreme rates typically get applied to initial and non monthly cleans due to growth
  • Boats outside of Panama City Beach will have a $25.00 fee for travel
