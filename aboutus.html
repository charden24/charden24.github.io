About Us

Hello! We are CJ and Nicki Harden! We have lived in Florida for years now, with our 4 dogs and more recently our son, Coast! We originally started this business to give people wonderful times out on the water and still continue to provide fun private boat tours! CJ has decided to re enter the maintenance side of things saying "I love giving people great experiences on the water, but I want to help the people who are stuck sweating on their docks or in thier driveways missing out on a great water day. Thus the maintenance division was born

About The Boat

The boat is a 1987 wellcraft 230 fisherman with a 150 honda. Funny story, we traded a four wheeler for the boat and reubilt the enire thing. After a bit of demo all that was left was the shell, console, and a few blue doors. Now it the vessel everyone has fun on and remembers because who can forget the pink boat!
